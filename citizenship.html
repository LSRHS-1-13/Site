Citizenship

My Contract

Citizenship is an important part of every day life, because the citizens make up the country and thus are the most important part of a country.
Honesty is an important part of being a citizen because lying leads to corruption which is a plague to nations.
Compassion is a neccesary part of being a good citizen because a country prospers when everyone helps eachother out in whatever way they can.
Having Respect for your peers allows you to learn from their sucesses and failiures and creates a more productive society.
Taking Responsibility for your duties creates less crime if everyone knows what they have to do and what will happen if they don't.
Having Courage allows citizens to stand up for themselves and their communities and is a vital part of being a citizen.
Another important trait of any citizen should be their Willingness To Serve their society and country in any way possible in order to improve the world.
One impressive act of citizenship is the stories of people Risking Their Lives to save others from the destructive power of hurricane Irma.
